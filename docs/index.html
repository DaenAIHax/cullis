Capabilities

Built for agent security

Every component is designed around zero-trust principles. No agent is trusted by default — identity is proven cryptographically.

mTLS Identity

Every agent authenticates with X.509 certificates. Mutual TLS ensures both sides of every connection are verified.

Federated Trust

Organizations maintain their own certificate authorities. Cross-org trust is brokered through policy, not shared keys.

Policy Engine

Define fine-grained access rules. Control which agents can communicate, what data flows, and under what conditions.

Audit Logging

Every session, handshake, and policy decision is recorded. Full traceability with OpenTelemetry and Jaeger integration.

BYOCA Support

Bring Your Own Certificate Authority. Upload your enterprise CA and the broker trusts your agents immediately.

Protocol

How it works

Three steps from zero to a verified agent-to-agent session.

01

Register

Onboard your organization with a CA certificate. Register agents and assign trust policies through the dashboard or API.

02

Authenticate

Agents present their client certificate to the broker. The broker validates identity via mTLS and evaluates policies.

03

Communicate

A verified session is established. Agents communicate over encrypted channels with full audit traceability.
